About

Ankita Saloni is currently a Fellow at Alphaa AI and a former Content-Writing Intern at Careers360. She is an Open Source Enthusiast and a Hackathon aficionado, who loves to volunteer, build and work to promote Under-represented groups in Technology. She is also the recipient of 21 Under 21 Awards'2020 by GirlScript for her meaningful contributions towards the society. She is also an author, public speaker, community builder and thespian who wishes to explore various domains of her interest and gain experiential knowledge in that.

Prior to the current role, she has been a Mentor to help beginners get started with their Open-Source journey through programs like StudentCode-In and Contributor's Hack and was an Open-source contributor for programs like GirlScript Summer of Code and Autumn of Codes by Sanscript. She has also interned with various organisations such as Careers360, Wrytin and Highway Connect in the domain of Content Writing and Digital Marketing. She is a Microsoft Learn Student Ambassador and #IAmRemarkable facilitator. She is currently persuing her Bachelors in Computer Science and Engineering from Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam Technical University and is an ex-alumni of University of Delhi(DU).
